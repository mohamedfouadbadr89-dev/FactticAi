# Advisory Board Charter: Facttic v3.7

## 1. Purpose
The Advisory Board provides independent oversight and strategic guidance on Facttic's technical integrity, risk management, and market expansion.

## 2. Composition
The board consists of three primary domains:
- **Security & Infrastructure**: Oversees V3.0 core stability and VPC isolation logic.
- **AI & Ethics**: Reviews Predictive Risk models (PRI) for bias and deterministic accuracy.
- **Compliance & Legal**: Ensures alignment with GDPR, SOC2, and Institutional SLA requirements.

## 3. Responsibilities
- **Quarterly Risk Review**: Evaluate PRI telemetry and structural drift metrics.
- **Incident Oversight**: Review any "Fail-CLOSED" events and provide third-party validation of remediation.
- **Strategic Roadmap**: Advise on multi-region expansion and sovereignty requirements.

## 4. Operational Rhythm
- **Meetings**: Once per fiscal quarter.
- **Reporting**: Board meeting summaries are archived as "Assurance Artifacts" for institutional clients.
