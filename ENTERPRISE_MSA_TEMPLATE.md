# Enterprise Master Services Agreement (MSA) - Facttic v3.7

## 1. Scope of Services
Facttic provides an Institutional-Grade AI Governance Layer, including:
- **Constitutional Core**: Hardened multi-tenant isolation and deterministic billing.
- **Predictive Governance**: PRI engine and infrastructure drift detection.
- **Institutional VPC**: Dedicated environment deployment and BYOK encryption support.

## 2. Institutional Guarantees
- **Data Sovereignty**: Service resolves identity strictly via JWT claims. No client-side organization headers are trusted.
- **Isolation Protection**: Row-Level Security (RLS) is active on 100% of data tables.
- **Resilience**: Fail-CLOSED policy for all rate-limiting and security layers.

## 3. Revenue & Billing
- **Determinism**: Billing is calculated based on atomic execution units (EU) as recorded in the locked v3.0 core.
- **Payment Terms**: [Net-30/Net-60] as specified in individual Statements of Work (SOW).
- **Renewal**: Automatic one-year renewals unless terminated with 90-day notice.

## 4. Governance & Compliance
- **Audit Rights**: The Client has the right to access RLS and Transactional audit logs via the Facttic interface.
- **Assurance**: Facttic provides Continuous Assurance reports and SOC2 Type I evidence packages.

## 5. Limitation of Liability
Facttic's liability is limited to the fees paid by the Client in the [12] months preceding the incident.
