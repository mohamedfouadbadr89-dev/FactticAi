# Important Update: Facttic v1.0 Launch Timeline Adjustment

**To:** All Facttic Teams (Engineering, QA, Security, Product, Support)
**Date:** March 2026

Team,

I wanted to share an important update regarding our launch timeline. After careful consideration and discussions with the leadership team, we've decided to push back the public launch by one week.

I know you've all been working incredibly hard to get Facttic ready for the big day, and this decision is not a reflection on your efforts. Rather, it's a strategic move to ensure we deliver the best possible product to our users.

Here are the key reasons behind the one-week delay:

**End-to-End Testing:** We want to conduct additional rounds of comprehensive testing on a production-like environment, particularly focusing on the new voice capabilities and webhook integrations. This will help us identify and address any last-minute issues or performance bottlenecks.

**Security and Compliance:** We're taking some extra time to thoroughly review our GDPR and SOC2 compliance posture, and remediate any potential vulnerabilities in our application and infrastructure. This due diligence is critical to protect our users' data and maintain their trust.

**Performance Monitoring:** We're setting up a robust monitoring and alerting system to ensure we can proactively detect and respond to any performance issues in the production environment. This will help us maintain a smooth experience for our users, even under heavy load.

**User Documentation and Support Readiness:** We're investing in creating comprehensive user guides, tutorials, and knowledge base articles to help our users get started with Facttic quickly and easily. We're also training our support team to handle any inquiries or issues that may arise post-launch.

Over the next week, we'll be focusing on these critical areas to ensure a successful and stable launch. We'll also be conducting a series of limited pilots with selected customers to gather early feedback and validate our readiness.

I want to emphasize that this short delay will only make our product stronger and more reliable. It's a testament to our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Please continue with your current work streams, and if you have any questions or concerns, don't hesitate to reach out to me or your team lead. Let's use this extra time wisely and cross the finish line together!

Thank you for your understanding and continued dedication. I'm incredibly proud of what we've accomplished so far, and I can't wait to see the impact Facttic will make in the world.

Onward and upward,

**The Leadership Team**
