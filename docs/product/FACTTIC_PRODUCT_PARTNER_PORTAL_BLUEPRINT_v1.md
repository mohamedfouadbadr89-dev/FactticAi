# Facttic Partner Portal Blueprint (v3.9)

## 1. Objective
A dedicated, self-service platform for Strategic Partners (Fortune 500) and Managed Service Providers (MSPs) to govern their Facttic deployments.

## 2. Global Fleet Management
- **VPC Dashboard**: Real-time health and PRI telemetry across multiple partner-managed VPCs.
- **Resource Control**: One-click vertical scaling for isolated database nodes.
- **Multi-Region Toggle**: Deploy new clusters in any supported Facttic region (US, EU, APAC).

## 3. Provisioning & Licensing
- **Self-Service VPC**: Automated infra spawning for new internal business units.
- **License Management**: Dashboard for seat allocation and institutional tier expansion.
- **Billing Console**: Aggregated view of EU consumption across all partner-owned organizations.

## 4. Governance & Trust
- **Audit Sync**: Batch export of RLS and Audit logs across the entire partner fleet.
- **Compliance Vault**: Direct access to SOC2 packages and regional sovereignty certifications.
- **White-Label Control**: Custom CSS/Logo injection for large internally-facing deployments.

## 5. Support Integration
- **Platinum Tunnel**: Direct Slack/PagerDuty integration for partner NOCs.
- **Incident Escalation**: Manage root-cause reports (RCAs) generated by the `IncidentAnalyzer`.
